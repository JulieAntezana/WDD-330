    Floral ID App

    Overview

    Do you enjoy floral bouquets? Have you thought about creating your own floral arrangements? Not sure where to begin? Then this app is for you. You will learn to identify the flowers that are used in your favorite designs. You'll be able to name your favorite designs and save them for later. Read the descriptions to learn which flowers are in the arrangement. If you are unfamiliar with a flower, click the Flowers List to learn more. (Links to external website. Right click to open link in new tab.)
